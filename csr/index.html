About Us

Corporate Social Responsibility, for everyone at UPES ACM & ACM-W Student Chapters, is the integration of Socially Beneficial Programs & Practices with a particular Corporation's Business Model and Culture. We give back by establishing activities that can help forge a stronger bond between the Corporate Circuit and the Rest of the World. We aim to increase long-term relationships with the Society by enabling the Socio-Economically Weaker Sections, Enable them and make them Future-Ready.

A Good Environment = Good Company + Good People;

Our Vision

To optimize the homogeneity in the Amalgamation of Societally Benefitting Efforts and a Sustainable future for UPES ACM & ACM-W. To Engage with more Organizations with a similar Vision and form meaningful Unions to spread the word about the Computing World!

Be a part of the network which is recognised globally, and provide learning-aids and opportunities for innovation internationally to revolutionalize transformations.

Our Goal

Students at the Chapter participate in C.S.R. activities to help maintain consistency in their Work Ethic and do their bit for the Community.
Our Calenders are marked with developmental sessions to provide help for the ones in need by making them realize Equality in terms of Education.

Discipline, Respect, and A Passion for Excellent Provision are just some qualities that the Team possesses.

Make A Difference

The MAD CSR was conducted at Christian Retreat Center. The activity was successfully performed among 30 kids aged 12-13. It was an initiative to help those underprivileged children to bring them into the mainstream of knowledge and, at least, build up their interest in science, which they are still not aware of.

Make A Difference(MAD) is a youth driven, non-profit organization working to ensure equitable outcomes for children in orphanages and street shelters. The CSR team, along with the PR team, comprising of the following members:-

promoted an online fundraiser started by the Make-A-Difference foundation for the campaign for a children’s shelter at Rajpur Road. The CSR team visited the campaign for the same on the 1st of April 2018 including, some fun and technical activities to have an enjoyable, creative and interactive session with the kids. We had a project based activity there. Activity was mainly to make a quizboard using electricity. Children were first told about the materials required, explaining each material and its use specifically. After that they were guided, step-by-step, how to proceed and ultimately we came out with an excellent project.

But, as always, it has never been about the completing the project. It was and is all about making those children understand the importance of science and showing them the path of technology and building up their interests so they might find it easier to figure it out what they should go for because we understand that even the most vulnerable children in shelters are capable to create magic.
